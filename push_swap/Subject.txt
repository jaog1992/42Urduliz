 STACK A | STACK B

 STACK A has random amount of +/- non duplicated numbers
 STACK B is empty

 sa (swap a): Swap the first 2 elements at the top of stack A (do nothing if only 1 or none elements)
 sb (swap a): Swap the first 2 elements at the top of stack B (do nothing if only 1 or none elements)
 ss : sa and sb at the same time

 pa (push a): Takes first element at the top of B and puts it at the top of A
 pb (push b): Takes first element at the top of A and puts it at the top of B

 ra (rotate a): Shift up al elements of stack A by 1 (First element becomes the last one)
 rb (rotate b): Shift up al elements of stack B by 1 (First element becomes the last one)
 rr : ra and rb at the same time

 rra (reverse rotate a): Shift down all elements of stack A by 1. (The last element becomes the first one)
 rrb (reverse rotate b): Shift down all elements of stack B by 1. (The last element becomes the first one)
 rrr : rra and rrb at the same time

Instructions must be separated by a '\n' and nothing else
If no parameters are specified, the program must not display anything and give the prompt back.
In case of error it must display "Error" followed by a '\n' (Errors: not integers, lower or bigger or duplicates)
